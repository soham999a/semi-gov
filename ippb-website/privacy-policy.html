1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you open an account or use our services, we collect:

  • Full name, date of birth, gender
  • Mobile number and email address
  • Residential address, city, state, pincode
  • Aadhaar number (encrypted and masked)
  • PAN card number (if provided)
  • Account type and banking preferences

Transaction Data

We automatically collect data about your transactions including amounts, dates, merchant details, and transaction types for account management and fraud prevention.

Technical Data

We collect device information, IP addresses, browser type, and usage patterns to improve our services and ensure security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Open and manage your bank account
  • Process transactions and service requests
  • Verify your identity (KYC compliance)
  • Send transaction alerts and account notifications
  • Comply with RBI, UIDAI and other regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and ensure account security
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Respond to your queries and complaints

We will never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Aadhaar Data Protection

IPPB complies strictly with the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016 and all UIDAI guidelines:

  • Aadhaar numbers are never stored in plain text — they are encrypted using AES-256
  • Only the last 4 digits are displayed in any interface
  • Aadhaar data is used solely for authentication and KYC as permitted by law
  • Biometric data is never stored — it is used only for real-time authentication
  • We do not share Aadhaar data with any third party except as required by law

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Government authorities: RBI, UIDAI, Income Tax Department as required by law
  • Payment networks: NPCI for UPI and AEPS transactions
  • Service providers: Technology partners under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement: When required by court order or legal process

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any commercial third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with CERT-In guidelines
  • Automatic session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity
  • All admin actions are logged in immutable audit trails

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: For the duration of the account plus 10 years (as per RBI guidelines)
  • Transaction records: 10 years from transaction date
  • KYC documents: 5 years after account closure
  • Audit logs: 7 years

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ippb.gov.in or visit our service center.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — for session management and security. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.
