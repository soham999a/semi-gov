About India Post Payments Bank

Bringing banking to every doorstep — trusted by millions across India since 2018.

Who We Are

India's Most Accessible Bank

India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) was established under the Department of Posts, Ministry of Communications, Government of India. It was incorporated as a Public Limited Company on 17th August 2016 and commenced operations on 1st September 2018.

IPPB has been set up with the vision to build the most accessible, affordable and trusted bank for the common man. It leverages the vast postal network of over 1.5 lakh post offices and 3 lakh postal employees to deliver banking services at the doorstep of every Indian.

  • Incorporated under the Companies Act 2013
  • Licensed by Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
  • 100% owned by Government of India
  • Operates through Department of Posts network
Our Mission

Banking for Every Indian, Everywhere

IPPB's mission is to remove barriers to financial inclusion by providing simple, affordable and accessible banking services to all citizens, especially those in rural and semi-urban areas who have historically been underserved by traditional banks.

  • Financial inclusion for the last mile
  • Doorstep banking through Postmen and GDS
  • Paperless, presence-less, cashless banking
  • Interoperable with all banks via UPI and AEPS
  • Support for all government DBT schemes
