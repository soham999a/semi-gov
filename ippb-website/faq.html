Account Opening
You can open an IPPB account by visiting our service center with your Aadhaar card and mobile number. The process is completely paperless and takes less than 5 minutes. You can also register online through this website.
You only need:
  • Aadhaar Card (for KYC verification)
  • Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
  • PAN Card (optional, for higher transaction limits)
No physical documents need to be submitted.
No. IPPB offers zero-balance savings accounts. There is no minimum balance requirement. You can open and maintain an account with zero balance.
As per RBI guidelines for Payments Banks, the maximum balance at end of day is Rs. 2,00,000 (Two Lakh Rupees). This limit may be revised by RBI from time to time.
AEPS Services
AEPS (Aadhaar Enabled Payment System) allows you to perform banking transactions using your Aadhaar number and fingerprint biometric. You don't need a debit card or PIN. Simply provide your Aadhaar number and authenticate with your fingerprint at our center.
Through AEPS you can:
  • Cash withdrawal from any bank account
  • Balance enquiry
  • Mini statement
  • Aadhaar to Aadhaar fund transfer
  • Receive Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) payments
Government Schemes
To receive PM Kisan Samman Nidhi through IPPB, you need to link your IPPB account with your PM Kisan registration. Visit our center with your Aadhaar and PM Kisan registration number. We will help you seed your IPPB account for direct benefit transfer.
Yes. We assist with enrollment and account seeding for Old Age Pension, Widow Pension, Disability Pension under NSAP, and Atal Pension Yojana. Visit our center with your Aadhaar and relevant documents.
Security & Safety
Yes. IPPB is licensed by RBI and 100% owned by the Government of India. Your deposits are insured up to Rs. 5 Lakhs under DICGC (Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation). All transactions are secured with 256-bit encryption.

Transactions & Limits
  • Daily transaction limit: Rs. 1,00,000
  • Monthly transaction limit: Rs. 2,00,000
  • Single AEPS withdrawal: Rs. 10,000
  • UPI per transaction: Rs. 1,00,000
Limits may vary based on KYC status and account type.
IMPS transfers are instant (24/7). NEFT transfers are processed in batches and typically complete within 2 hours during banking hours. RTGS is for amounts above Rs. 2 Lakhs and is near-instant during banking hours.
