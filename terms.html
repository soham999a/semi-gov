Terms of Service

Please read these terms carefully before using IPPB services

Last updated: 1 January 2025 | Effective Date: 1 January 2025

By opening an account or using IPPB services, you agree to these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Eligibility

To open an IPPB account, you must:

  • Be a resident Indian citizen
  • Be at least 18 years of age (minors may open accounts with guardian)
  • Possess a valid Aadhaar number linked to your mobile number
  • Not be an existing IPPB account holder (one account per person)

2. Account Terms

Account Limits

  • Maximum end-of-day balance: Rs. 2,00,000 as per RBI guidelines
  • Daily transaction limit: Rs. 1,00,000
  • IPPB does not offer credit facilities or loans

Account Maintenance

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials. You must immediately notify us of any unauthorized access or suspicious activity.

Account Closure

IPPB reserves the right to close accounts that are dormant for more than 24 months, used for fraudulent activities, or in violation of these terms.

3. Acceptable Use

You agree NOT to use IPPB services for:

  • Money laundering or financing of illegal activities
  • Transactions involving prohibited goods or services
  • Providing false information during registration or KYC
  • Unauthorized access to other accounts
  • Any activity that violates applicable Indian laws

4. OTP and Security

You must never share your OTP, PIN, or password with anyone — including IPPB staff. IPPB will never ask for your OTP or password. You are solely responsible for transactions authorized using your credentials.

IPPB will NEVER ask for your OTP, PIN, or full Aadhaar number over phone or email. Beware of fraudsters impersonating IPPB.

5. Transaction Disputes

For disputed transactions, you must report within 3 days of the transaction date. IPPB will investigate and resolve within 30 days as per RBI guidelines. For fraud-related disputes, call our 24/7 helpline immediately.

6. Service Availability

IPPB services are available 24/7 through digital channels. Physical service center hours are Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We may temporarily suspend services for maintenance with prior notice where possible.

7. Fees and Charges

Account opening and basic banking services are free. Specific service charges (if any) are displayed before transaction confirmation. IPPB reserves the right to revise charges with 30 days prior notice.
